# Data Notes

The competition data is downloaded from Kaggle and is not stored in this repository.

Local inspection on 2026-07-01:

- Train rows: 690,088
- Test rows: 295,753
- Target: `health_condition`
- Classes: `at-risk`, `fit`, `unhealthy`
- Metric: balanced accuracy
- Daily submission limit: 10

The dataset is inspired by the public College Student Health Behavior Dataset, but the competition train and test distributions are synthetic and include missing values.
